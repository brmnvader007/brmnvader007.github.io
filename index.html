What is ketosis?

Ketosis means that the body uses fats for energy instead of carbohydrates. It occurs when there are very few carbohydrates in the diet, usually less than 50 grams a day. Fats are essential for survival because every cell in the body can use them as an energy source. In fact, fats are vital to many biological processes in the body and are even used as structural material for cell walls and components of the cells themselves.

The ketogenic diet plan forces the body to burn fat, not carbohydrates. When fats are burned, the body produces chemicals called ketones, which can be used as energy by healthy brain and body cells.
